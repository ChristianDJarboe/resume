First Blog Post

Ludoviqa is a company that was created based on a passion for fashion, nature, culture and helping. The goal for this brand is to combine traditional attire from around the world with current fashion trends. We try to create a wide variety of styles and sizes for all. With Ludoviqa we try to not only bring awareness to current events and wild life, but we

also want to help. With each purchase a percentage will go towards the charity for that collection. Not only that but all packaging is made of recycled materials and all clothing is hand made and provides more jobs to the people of Mexico. We hope to not only make a change but we hope you will join us on this journey.
